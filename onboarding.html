🚀 Getting Started

1
Download the Setup Script
Click the download button below to get the onboarding script to your computer.
2
Run as Administrator
Right-click the downloaded file and select "Run as Administrator". This is required for device setup.
3
Follow the Setup Process
The script will automatically configure all your Microsoft 365 services. This may take a few minutes.
4
Restart Your Device
After setup completes, restart your computer for all changes to take effect.
